Welcome to Planet Protectors

Planet Protectors is an app designed to encourage eco-friendly behavior through fun and engaging nature challenges. Complete challenges, upload photos, and earn badges while making a positive impact on our planet.

Tips to Help the Planet

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle
  • Conserve water and energy
  • Plant trees and support reforestation
  • Use sustainable transportation options
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Support local and organic produce
